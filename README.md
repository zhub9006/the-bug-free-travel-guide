# 🚫🪲 Bug-Free Travel Guide

> *A community-driven guide for bug-phobic travelers — discovering the world's safest, most insect-free destinations with seasonal advice and travel tips.*

## ✨ About This Project

Are you terrified of bugs? Do creepy-crawlies make you break out in hives at the thought of backyard barbecues? You're not alone — and you **deserve** to see the world without worrying about every flying, crawling thing that might cross your path.

This repo is built by bug-phobes, for bug-phobes. We research and curate destinations around the world that are naturally bug-free or have remarkably low insect populations, and provide seasonal advice so you can plan your trip when the chances of encountering pests are slim to none.

## 🤝 How to Contribute

We welcome contributions from fellow bug-phobes! Here's how you can help:

1. **Add a destination** — Create a new guide in `DESTINATIONS/` following our [contribution template](DESTINATIONS/_template.md)
2. **Verify claims** — Do you have firsthand experience or reliable data about a bug-free destination? Share it!
3. **Update seasonal info** — Climate patterns change; help us keep the guides current
4. **Share tips** — Bug-repelling gear, hotel recommendations, or local knowledge

### Contribution Guidelines
- Destinations must have **documented low or zero insect populations**
- Include **seasonal recommendations** with specific months
- Provide **practical tips** for avoiding the few insects that do exist
- Cite **reliable sources** where possible

## 📊 What Makes a Destination "Bug-Free"?

We evaluate destinations based on:
- **Mosquito presence** (the #1 concern for most travelers)
- **Fly and ant populations**
- **Spider and tick density**
- **Overall insect biodiversity**
- **Climate factors** (humidity, temperature, rainfall)
- **Government pest control measures**

## ⚠️ Disclaimer

No destination is 100% guaranteed bug-free at all times. Always check current conditions before traveling and pack appropriate preventative measures. This guide is based on research and community reports but individual experiences may vary.
